We Believe

  • that Jesus Christ is the one and only Son of God who died for our sins and arose from the dead. 1 Corinthians 15:1–8
  • that forgiveness of sins and the promise of eternal life are available for those who trust Christ as Savior and Lord. John 3:16
  • that those accepting Christ should repent of sin, confess their faith and be baptized into Him. Romans 10:9, Acts 2:38
  • that Jesus Christ will one day return and reign forever as King of Kings and Lord of Lords. 1 Thessalonians 4:13–18
